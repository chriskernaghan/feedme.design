Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start?

Usually within a day or two. I'll run a free audit of your site, walk you through what I find, and if we're a good fit, I can start that same week. No onboarding decks, no kickoff meetings — just straight into the work.

What does the monthly retainer actually include?

Core, plugin and theme updates, daily backups, uptime monitoring, security scanning and a set number of support hours each month. If something breaks at 9pm on a Tuesday, I'll deal with it. You get a monthly report so you can see exactly what's been done.

Am I locked into a contract?

No. It's a rolling monthly arrangement — you can cancel any time. I'd rather keep clients because the work speaks for itself, not because of a contract clause.

Do you only work with WordPress sites?

WordPress is my specialism, but I've migrated clients from Squarespace, Wix and other platforms. If you're on something else and want to move to WordPress, that's something I can help with too.

What if I need something outside my included hours?

No problem — extra work is billed at a straightforward hourly rate and I'll always check with you before going over. No surprise invoices.

Can you help with analytics and tracking?

Yes. I set up GA4 and Google Tag Manager for clients regularly — form submissions, phone clicks, CTA tracking, the lot. You'll actually know what's working on your site instead of guessing.

I'm not very technical — is that a problem?

Not at all. Most of my clients aren't. I explain things in plain English and handle everything on the technical side. You just need to tell me what you need — I'll take care of how.

How do I get in touch if something's urgent?

Email or phone — whichever's easier. Response time is typically under an hour during working hours, and I keep an eye on things outside of them too. You're not going into a ticket queue.
