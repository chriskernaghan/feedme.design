// the whole file, one place

The admin that fills your evenings, handled before lunch.

Counsel keeps the discipline of a well-run case file — without the paper, the spreadsheets, or the chasing.

Open a matter in seconds

Web enquiries become structured files automatically — client details, practice area, and a reference assigned the moment someone gets in touch.

Deadlines that chase themselves

Key dates, limitation periods, and follow-ups tracked per matter. Counsel reminds the right person before anything slips.

Conflict checks, instant

Every new party is checked against your full history before you take the work on. Clear in one click, evidenced for compliance.

Time and billing, connected

Record time against the matter as you work. Draft bills assemble themselves from what's already logged — no month-end scramble.

Client onboarding done right

ID verification, engagement letters, and e-signatures handled in the flow. The client experience small firms rarely have time to build.

Audit trail by default

Who did what, and when, recorded on every file. Reassuring for your regulator, invaluable when a matter is ever questioned.
