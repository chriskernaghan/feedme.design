<CaseSection label="The Challenge" heading="Hitting the Limits of Squarespace">
Catagen came to us with a problem many growing businesses face: they had outgrown their website platform. Their Squarespace site had served them well in the early days, but as their team expanded and their technical requirements became more sophisticated, the limitations became clear.

- Limited flexibility for custom functionality and integrations
- Difficulty scaling the site as the business grew
- Team needed more control over content and structure
- Wanted a solution that could evolve with the business without requiring a rebuild every few years

They needed a platform that could handle their current requirements while remaining flexible enough to accommodate future growth, without sacrificing the ease of use their team had come to expect.
</CaseSection>

<CaseSection label="The Solution" heading="Enterprise WordPress, Done Right">
We rebuilt Catagen's entire web presence from the ground up on WordPress, hosted on WP Engine, a managed WordPress platform known for enterprise-grade performance, security, and reliability.

The approach was straightforward but comprehensive:

<ul class="solution-tiles">
<li><strong>Custom theme development</strong>Built a bespoke WordPress theme tailored to Catagen's brand and technical requirements.</li>
<li><strong>Divi integration</strong>Incorporated Divi page builder to give the Catagen team intuitive, drag-and-drop editing capabilities without sacrificing code quality.</li>
<li><strong>WP Engine hosting</strong>Migrated to WP Engine for automatic backups, staging environments, and enterprise-level security.</li>
<li><strong>Performance optimisation</strong>Configured caching, CDN, and image optimisation from day one.</li>
<li><strong>Security hardening</strong>Implemented security best practices, monitoring, and regular updates.</li>
</ul>

The goal was to give Catagen the best of both worlds: the power and flexibility of WordPress with the ease of use they had on Squarespace.
</CaseSection>
