Frequently asked

Questions, answered plainly.

If you don't see what you're looking for, get in touch with our team and we'll come back to you the same week.

What is CATAGEN and what does the company do?
CATAGEN is a clean-tech company developing technologies that accelerate the transition to net zero. We deliver three complementary capabilities: Green Emissions Testing for OEMs, Sustainable Aviation Fuel production via Green Flight, and clean-energy reactor systems through ClimaHtech.
What industries do you work with?
Automotive OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers, aerospace and aviation, defence, energy producers, and industrial decarbonisation programmes.
What is Green Emissions Testing?
A patented method for accelerated catalyst and emissions system testing that delivers OEM-grade results faster and at lower cost than conventional engine-based testing.
What is ClimaHtech and how does it relate to CATAGEN?
ClimaHtech is our reactor portfolio — covering hydrogen generation (BioHGen), e-fuel production (e-Fuel Gen) and compression technology — for clean energy infrastructure.
Does CATAGEN offer support for emissions compliance?
Yes. We work with OEMs and regulators globally on emissions compliance pathways, supported by our testing infrastructure and engineering team.
Where is CATAGEN located?
Headquartered in Belfast, Northern Ireland, with deployments and partners across 11 countries.
