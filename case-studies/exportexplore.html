ExportExplore: A Fresh Start for a Growing Business

How we rebuilt an outdated website that was losing leads into a professional platform that reflects the scale and professionalism of the business.

Client
ExportExplore
Industry
International Trade
Timeline
4 weeks initial build
Ongoing
2+ years
The Challenge

An Outdated Site Holding Back Growth

ExportExplore came to us with a problem: their website no longer reflected the business they had become. As the company scaled and their services evolved, the outdated site was actively working against them.

  • Website design and messaging didn't match the professionalism of the business
  • Lost leads because prospects couldn't see the full scope of services offered
  • Founder recognized the gap between their capabilities and how they were presenting online
  • Site had become difficult to update and maintain

They needed a complete fresh start — not just visual updates, but a rebuild that could grow with the business and accurately represent what ExportExplore had become.

The Solution

A Modern WordPress Site Built for Growth

We rebuilt ExportExplore's entire web presence from the ground up, working closely with their team to create a site that truly represents their expertise in international trade.

  • Fresh design: Modern, professional aesthetic that reflects the scale of the business
  • Elementor page builder: Client had experience with Elementor, so we built with it to give them comfortable editing capabilities
  • Local hosting partner: Set up with a reliable local hosting company for performance and support
  • Performance optimization: Fast load times and clean code from day one
  • Security hardening: Implemented security best practices and monitoring

The goal was to give ExportExplore a platform that accurately represents their expertise while remaining easy for their team to manage and update.
