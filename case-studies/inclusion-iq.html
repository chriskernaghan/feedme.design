The Challenge

A Multi-Service Brand That Needed Room to Breathe

InclusionIQ isn't a single-service business. It's a recruitment agency, a consultancy, a training provider, and the home of The She Suite — a dedicated programme for female leadership and career advancement. All of that needed to live on one website without feeling fragmented or overwhelming.

  • Multiple distinct service lines competing for attention on the homepage
  • Job listings that needed to be easy to browse by sector — Tech, HR, Management and beyond
  • The She Suite needed to feel like a flagship initiative, not a buried page
  • The team needed to update content and post jobs without calling a developer every time
  • A platform reliable enough to handle traffic from candidates, employers and corporate clients alike

The brief came down to one thing: a site that could hold everything InclusionIQ does without making visitors work to find what they came for.

The Solution

One Platform, Multiple Audiences

We built InclusionIQ on WordPress, hosted on WP Engine — a managed platform built for performance, security and reliability at scale. The structure was designed to give each audience a clear path through the site without competing for the same space.

Key pieces of the build:

  • Custom theme development: A bespoke WordPress theme designed around InclusionIQ's brand, with distinct visual treatments for each service area
  • Job listings architecture: Categorised by sector with clear calls to action for both candidates ("Find a Job") and employers ("Hire your next hire")
  • The She Suite as a flagship section: Treated as a destination in its own right, with space to grow as the programme expands
  • WP Engine hosting: Automatic backups, staging environments, and enterprise-level security baked in
  • Performance & security: Caching, CDN, image optimisation and security hardening configured from day one
  • Editorial control: The InclusionIQ team can publish jobs, update consultancy content and add new programmes without touching code

The aim was to give InclusionIQ a site that works as hard as the team behind it — fast, reliable, and easy to keep current.

The Outcome

A Site That Reflects the Ambition Behind the Brand

The new InclusionIQ platform doesn't just present the business — it works with the team to keep candidates, employers and corporate clients moving through the site without friction.

Built for Multiple Audiences

Candidates, employers and corporate clients each get a clear route into the parts of the site that matter to them — without sacrificing the cohesion of the brand.

Editorial Independence

The InclusionIQ team can post jobs, update programme details and publish new content without waiting on a developer. Speed without compromise.

The She Suite, Front & Centre

A flagship treatment for the female leadership programme — designed as a destination, not an afterthought, with room to grow as the initiative expands.

Reliable Foundations

WP Engine hosting, automated backups, security monitoring and ongoing maintenance mean the InclusionIQ team can focus on their work — not WordPress.

Built for Every Device

The site is fully responsive across desktop, tablet and mobile — meeting candidates wherever they are, whether browsing job listings on a phone during a commute or exploring corporate services on a laptop.

Navigation adapts intelligently to the device, keeping every service area within easy reach without cluttering the experience.

Today, InclusionIQ has a website that holds every part of the business in one place — and a foundation that's ready for whatever the team builds next.
